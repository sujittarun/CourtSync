A read-only availability board. Bookings arrive from the channel partners (Playo · Hudle · District) and your website — a slot taken on any channel is blocked on all of them. Tap a coloured cell to see who holds it. Nothing is entered by hand here; everything is driven by the partner APIs and the ledger.
